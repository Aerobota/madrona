Several apps were created for MarineMap 2.0 on the north coast of California in support of the Marine Life Protection Act. These apps are no longer supported but can be ported to the madrona platform with minimal effort:

depth_range
geographic_report
intersection
pg_spacing
replication
straightline_spacing
spacing

They are still available at http://code.google.com/p/marinemap/ and http://code.google.com/p/northcoast-marinemap/
